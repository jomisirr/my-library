About Our Library

Our Story

Founded in 2025, My Local Library began as a high school web development project. What started as a simple idea has grown into a beautiful, fully offline digital bookshelf used by students around the world.

We believe that reading should be free, private, and accessible — even without an internet connection.

Our Mission

To build the simplest, fastest, and most beautiful personal library that works 100% offline, using only your browser.
